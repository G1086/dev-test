The Benefits of CommunityLiving

Studies have shown that 90% of time at home is spent occupying less than 40% of the space. It just doesnʼt make sense. Why not leverage all that unused space in a combined and efficient way for the of benefit of a whole community? That increased shared space also offers added benefits in providing just a few of this impressive range of features.

Relax & Revive - Roof decks

Roof spaces frequently tend to gounused in Australian homes, which is strange considering our fantastic weather, their size, space and natural light opportunities.

Wide Open Wellness

Adding together what ordinarily would be a metre of lawn in a Sydney property, creates one mighty Health & Well-Beingʼ lawn in the MicroVillas. As well as this Activity Spaces are also created.

Cutting Edge Communities

Added space allows for added value in technology terms. The Micro Villas provide IT & Learning Centres. This allows residents to work from home or pursue education opportunities.

Combining Convenience

Combined space also means access to larger and better quality amenities such as Mailing and Laundry hubs. All the convenience of laundromat right on your doorstep.
